EASY REMIND

Description

What's harder than actually keeping up with your notifications?

I've used Trello, ToDoist, the pomodoro method, and more, but still I get stuck with the things I want to accomplish and get drowned in stuff I haven't finished. Kanban is overwhelming both for myself and a close friend, and so I decided to build an easier messaging system that holds us accountable to our tasks!
